Brand Identity Exploration

Four directions for the ZB Automations family

Matrix Dashboard and Matrix Builder are siblings under one umbrella. Each direction below gives the family a complete, buildable identity — parent mark, two sub-marks, color and type systems, icon sets, social banners, README treatment, and applied UI — with its own typographic personality and construction logic.
